# 09. Path to Levels 102 and 103

Level 101 gives you baseline execution confidence. The next levels deepen engineering and operations.

## Promotion Criteria to Level 102

- You can train, evaluate, and deploy without step-by-step prompts.
- You understand data/model/environment versioning.
- You can explain endpoint behavior and basic monitoring signals.

## Level 102 Focus (Planned)

- Pipeline modularization and reusable components.
- Validation strategy and experiment governance.
- CI/CD integration for model release flows.

## Level 103 Focus (Planned)

- Enterprise security, policy, and compliance controls.
- Advanced reliability patterns and rollback automation.
- Portfolio-level governance and observability.

## Suggested Sequencing

1. Finish all Level 101 modules with hands-on completion.
2. Convert one manual workflow into a repeatable pipeline.
3. Move to Level 102 with measured operational goals.
